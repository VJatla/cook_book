Overview

  • Difficulty: Very easy
  • Time: 45 to 50 minutes
  • Cleaning effort: Very easy
  • Good with: Soup, tangy dal (tomato, mango, etc) and rasam.

Ingredients

  • Tilapia fish fillets (frozen works too).
  • Lemon juice
  • Red chilli powder
  • Turmeric
  • Salt
  • Ginger + garlic paste

Preparation

  1. Add lemon juice to tilapia fillets. Mix them well.
  2. All all the spices and evently coat them.
  3. Keep aside for 30 minutes for marination.
  4. Take a wide pan (cast iron is perfet!), add oil and heat it in medium heat.
  5. Fry on both sides of tilapia fillets until they are red, and have small charred brown spots on each side.
  6. Enjoy!
