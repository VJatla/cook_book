## Slow cooker
1. Very simple:
   + Servings: 2
   + Time: 10 hours
   + Cooker: Slow cooker at slowest setting
   + Propotions:
   ```
   1/2c*(moong dal) + 1/2c*(rice) + 2T*(ghee) + 4.5c*(water) 
   ```
