Chakkara pongali (sweet pongali)

  1. Roast dry fruits + dry coconut (optional, but very nice to have) in ghee.
  2. Dry roast 1/4 th cup of moong dal.
  3. Add it to 3/4 cup of rice in a instant pot.
  4. Add 2 and 1/2 cup of water.
  5. Cook the rice in "rice setting".
  6. Add 1 cup of jaggery and mix in low heat.
  7. Add dry fruits and serve.
